About Us

Welcome to MoneyTransfers, your trusted partner for sending money from Oman to East Africa. Our mission is to help the diaspora community send money affordably, quickly, and securely.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, we recognized the need for a reliable comparison platform specifically for money transfers from Oman to East African countries. Many people were paying excessive fees or experiencing delays with their transfers due to lack of information.

What We Do

We provide transparent comparisons of the top money transfer services, helping you find the best exchange rates, lowest fees, and fastest transfer times. We partner with trusted providers like Ria, Remitly, WorldRemit, Wise, and MoneyGram to ensure your money reaches its destination safely.

Our Values

  • Transparency: We provide clear information about fees and exchange rates
  • Security: We only recommend regulated and trusted money transfer providers
  • Value: We help you find the best deals to maximize your money's value
  • Community: We're committed to serving the Oman to East Africa diaspora community
