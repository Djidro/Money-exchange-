Best Way to Send Money from Oman to Rwanda

Posted on January 15, 2023

When sending money from Oman to Rwanda, you want to ensure your funds arrive quickly, securely, and with minimal fees. After comparing various services, we've found that the best options depend on your specific needs...

For best exchange rates: Wise typically offers the most competitive rates with transparent pricing.

For low fees: Remitly often has promotional offers for first-time users and lower fees for larger transfers.

How to Save on Remittance Fees

Posted on February 3, 2023

Remittance fees can significantly reduce the amount your family receives. Here are practical tips to save on transfer costs:

  • Compare exchange rates: Small differences in rates can add up to significant savings, especially for larger transfers.
  • Look for fee promotions: Many services offer reduced or waived fees for first-time users.
  • Choose slower transfer options: Express transfers often come with premium fees.
  • Send larger amounts less frequently: Many services charge flat fees that become a smaller percentage of your transfer when sending larger amounts.
  • Use online transfers instead of cash pickup: Digital transfers typically have lower fees than cash services.
