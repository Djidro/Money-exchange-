Frequently Asked Questions

Is my money safe?

Yes, all transfers are via trusted providers that are fully regulated and licensed. We only partner with established money transfer companies that have strong security measures in place to protect your funds and personal information.

How long does it take?

Transfer times vary by provider but are usually completed within minutes to a few hours. Some services may take 1-2 business days for bank transfers or first-time transactions that require additional verification.

What information do I need to send money?

Typically, you'll need:

  • Your valid identification
  • Recipient's full name
  • Recipient's location (city and country)
  • For bank transfers: recipient's bank account details
  • For cash pickup: recipient's phone number may be required
Are there limits on how much I can send?

Yes, most money transfer services have limits based on regulatory requirements and their own policies. These limits vary by provider and may depend on whether you're a new or existing customer, your verification level, and the transfer method. Typically, limits range from 1,000 OMR to 10,000 OMR per transaction.

What currencies can I send to East Africa?

You can send Omani Rial (OMR) which will be converted to the local currency of the destination country. Popular receiving currencies include:

  • Rwandan Franc (RWF) for Rwanda
  • Kenyan Shilling (KES) for Kenya
  • Ugandan Shilling (UGX) for Uganda
  • Tanzanian Shilling (TZS) for Tanzania
  • US Dollars (USD) are also widely accepted
